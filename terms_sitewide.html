What this site is

Living Beyond SCI is a personal project run by one person — Jeremy Olson. It offers free educational content related to spinal cord injury, including research summaries, lived-experience material, and self-guided practices.

Some sections of the site carry their own additional terms specific to the kind of work they involve. The Guided Imagery practices, for example, have their own additional notice covering the medical and interventional aspects of that work. Where that's the case, those terms appear on the relevant pages — please read them before you use those sections.

There are no subscriptions, no advertising, and no user accounts. The site does not collect personal information.
