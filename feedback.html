Your Experience

Share What You've Noticed

If you've been using the Guided Imagery Programme, I'd genuinely like to hear how it's been. There are no right answers. A few sentences is enough — or as much as you want to write.

This page is for anyone who's spent time with the practices — whether that's a few days or several months, whether something shifted or nothing did. Honest feedback is the only kind that helps the programme improve. You don't have to have had a positive experience to make it worth writing in.

If you'd rather write directly, you can reach me at jeremy@livingbeyondsci.com.

Also available

If you'd prefer to track your own experience privately — without sharing anything — there's a self-reflection journal you can download and fill in at your own pace. It's for your use only; nothing is submitted anywhere.
