Free resources for spinal cord injury

Living Beyond SCI

Living Beyond SCI is a free website for people living with spinal cord injury — and the people who care for them. It brings together three areas of work: hands-free harmonica playing for people with high-level injury, a guided imagery programme grounded in motor imagery research, and twenty-five years of quality of life evidence from rehabilitation psychology. The three connect. All of it is free, self-directed, and designed to be used from wherever you are.

01

Hands-Free Harmonica

A structured curriculum for people with high-level spinal cord injury. No hands required — just breath.

02

Guided Imagery Programme

Directed mental practice using motor imagery to stimulate nerve pathways. Evidence-based and self-directed.

03

Quality of Life Research

Twenty-five years of rehabilitation psychology evidence on what actually helps people flourish after SCI.

Harmonica trains the breath. Breath opens the door to imagery. Imagery changes the body and mind.
